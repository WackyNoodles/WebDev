Graphic Designer

I'm a passionate graphic designer with a penchant for creativity that extends far beyond the computer screen. When I'm not immersed in pixels and vectors, you'll often find me with a sketchbook in hand, capturing ideas and moments through drawing and sketching. Graphic design isn't just my profession; it's my playground where imagination runs wild. And when the night sky beckons, I trade my stylus for a telescope, losing myself in the beauty of the cosmos while indulging in the tranquil art of star gazing.
